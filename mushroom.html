Dangerous Species

Species Danger
Amanita phalloides (Death Cap) Highly toxic and can cause liver and kidney failure
Amanita virosa (Destroying Angel) Extremely toxic, often fatal if ingested
Death Cap Mushroom

This deadly mushroom is responsible for the most mushroom poisonings worldwide.

Destroying Angel Mushroom

A highly poisonous mushroom that can cause death within hours of ingestion.

Safe and Delicious Mushrooms

Not all mushrooms are dangerous! In fact, many mushrooms are delicious and nutritious. Here are some safe and tasty varieties:

  • Portobello – A hearty mushroom, often used as a meat substitute in vegetarian dishes.
  • Shiitake – A savory, umami-rich mushroom, commonly used in Asian cuisine.
  • Button Mushroom – The classic, versatile mushroom, great for soups, salads, and stir-fries.

Whether you love them for their earthy flavor or their health benefits, mushrooms are an exciting ingredient to experiment with in the kitchen.
