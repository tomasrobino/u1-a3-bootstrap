Health Benefits

Benefit Description
Low in Calories Spaghetti squash is very low in calories, making it a great alternative to pasta for those looking to reduce their calorie intake.
High in Fiber This veggie is a good source of fiber, which aids in digestion and helps maintain a healthy gut.
Spaghetti Squash

The vegetable that looks like pasta. Cut it open, scrape the insides, and you'll get spaghetti-like strands!

Cooked Spaghetti Squash

After cooking, the flesh of the squash can be scraped into long, noodle-like strands that can replace pasta.

Delicious Recipe Idea

Why not try a Spaghetti Squash Pasta with Marinara? Simply bake the squash, scrape out the strands, and top with your favorite marinara sauce, grilled vegetables, or even some meatballs for a hearty meal!

Spaghetti squash is a versatile vegetable that works well in many dishes, from savory to sweet, and it can be a great addition to your veggie repertoire.
